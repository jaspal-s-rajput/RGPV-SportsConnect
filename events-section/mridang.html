Description

The 23rd Mridang Championship is set to be the pinnacle of sportsmanship and athletic excellence this year at RGPV. This prestigious annual event has grown to become one of the most awaited competitions on campus, bringing together students from various academic disciplines who share a passion for sports. The Mridang Championship stands as a testament to the spirit of unity, dedication, and resilience among our young athletes. Participants not only display their skills but also embody the values of teamwork, discipline, and perseverance that the event seeks to nurture. The championship includes a wide spectrum of sports, ranging from intense track and field competitions to exhilarating team events. These sports aren’t just contests—they’re platforms for individuals and teams to challenge themselves, break personal limits, and strive for greatness. Each competition is meticulously organized to foster an environment that highlights both the competitive edge and the camaraderie that make sports so essential to student life. Athletes who participate in Mridang have a unique opportunity to represent their departments, colleges, or regions, all while honing their athletic abilities and embracing the principles of fair play and respect for opponents.

Beyond the thrill of the games, the Mridang Championship fosters an inclusive sports culture on campus. By promoting participation across all skill levels, it encourages every student with a love for sports to take part, ensuring that the event is as much about community and shared experiences as it is about winning. The championship provides a dynamic platform where new friendships are forged, teamwork is celebrated, and sportsmanship shines through every race, goal, or point scored. Spectators also play a vital role in the Mridang Championship, bringing a contagious energy that elevates the atmosphere. Cheering crowds motivate participants to push their limits and contribute to an unforgettable ambiance that stays with everyone involved long after the events conclude. The Mridang Championship isn’t just an event; it’s a celebration—a time when the campus comes alive with excitement and pride.

Join us at the 23rd Mridang Championship for a memorable journey of passion, perseverance, and achievement. Whether as a participant or a spectator, be part of an inspiring experience that blends competitive spirit with the warmth of fellowship. This championship promises to leave a lasting impact on everyone involved, fueling a shared commitment to excellence that transcends the boundaries of sports.

Sport Details & Eligibility

  • Sports Categories:BasketballVolleyballFootballBadmintonCricketCricket
  • Eligibility: Open to all (both Girls & Boys) registered university students with a valid student ID and Govt. ID.
  • Rules: Participants must adhere to university and national sports federation guidelines.

Venue & Staying Details

Location: RGPV Main Stadium, Bhopal

Please arrive at RGPV Main Stadium, Bhopal at the designated time for check-in and physical verification. All event activities, including matches, results, and the award ceremony, will take place here. Participants must also check out from this location after the event’s conclusion.

Staying Arrangements: Accommodation is available for outstation participants. Nearby hotels and university hostels are available and Booked.

For participants and guests needing accommodations, nearby hotels and guesthouses have been arranged and booked with special rates during the event. Assistance with room number and details will be available at the event registration desk. Please ensure to confirm your stay arrangements upon check-in at RGPV Main Stadium, Bhopal.

Event Timeline

  • Registration Deadline: March 10, 2025
  • Opening Ceremony: March 20, 2025, 10:00 AM
  • Preliminary Rounds: March 21-24, 2025
  • Semi-Finals: March 25, 2025
  • Finals: March 26, 2025
  • Award Ceremony: March 26, 2025, 6:00 PM

Rewards & Prizes

  • Champion: 1,00,000/- Cash Prize, Trophy & Gold Medal
  • 1st Runner-Up: 70,000/- Cash Prize & Silver Medal
  • 2nd Runner-Up: 40,000/- Cash Prize & Bronze Medal
  • Most-Valuable Player (MVP- 3 Players): 10,000/- Cash Prize & MVP Certificate
  • Fair Play Award (FPA - 5 Players): 5,000/- Cash Prize & FPA Certificate
  • All Participants: Certificate of Participation
