Description

The Brilliant Minds Chess Championship is an eagerly awaited event that brings together strategic thinkers and passionate chess enthusiasts from across the campus. This championship isn't just a contest of moves and strategies; it's a mental battleground where players demonstrate their intellectual prowess, patience, and problem-solving skills. As part of RGPV’s annual sports events, this chess championship celebrates the power of the mind, the beauty of strategy, and the spirit of competition.

Participants in the Brilliant Minds Chess Championship represent their academic departments or clubs, fostering a sense of pride and community. The championship features multiple rounds, testing players across levels of skill and resilience, as they navigate challenging opponents and evolving strategies. Each game offers participants a chance to refine their techniques, develop sharper focus, and experience the thrill of tactical wins and hard-fought draws. This event values not only individual achievement but also fair play and respect for fellow competitors. It creates a welcoming space for both seasoned chess players and newcomers, encouraging participation from a wide range of skill levels. The championship format allows each player to grow and learn through both victories and setbacks, reinforcing the values of perseverance and mental discipline.

Spectators are an integral part of the event, adding an enthusiastic backdrop that keeps players motivated. The quiet intensity of the games, the silent concentration of players, and the palpable excitement of the crowd come together to create a unique atmosphere that defines the Brilliant Minds Chess Championship. Join us for this intellectually stimulating experience, where every move counts, and every game is a journey of discovery. Whether you’re a participant or a spectator, the Brilliant Minds Chess Championship promises to be a memorable event filled with strategy, respect, and the shared love of the game.

Sport Details & Eligibility

  • Sports Categories:Chess
  • Eligibility: Open to all (both Girls & Boys) registered university students with a valid student ID and Govt. ID.
  • Rules: Participants must adhere to university and national sports federation guidelines.

Venue & Staying Details

Location: AITR Campus-Mangliya, Dewas Naka, Indore

Please arrive at AITR Campus-Mangliya, Indore at the designated time for check-in and physical verification. All event activities, including matches, results, and the award ceremony, will take place here. Participants must also check out from this location after the event’s conclusion.

Staying Arrangements: Accommodation is available for outstation participants. Nearby hotels and university hostels are available and Booked.

For participants and guests needing accommodations, nearby hotels and guesthouses have been arranged and booked with special rates during the event. Assistance with room number and details will be available at the event registration desk. Please ensure to confirm your stay arrangements upon check-in at AITR Campus-Mangliya, Indore.

Event Timeline

  • Preliminary Rounds: March 21-24, 2025

  • Finals: March 26, 2025
