Description

The Annual M-20 Athletic Sports Meet at RGPV is a grand celebration of athleticism, endurance, and sportsmanship, uniting students from diverse fields to compete and connect through their shared love for sports. This eagerly awaited event has become a symbol of excellence, discipline, and the unwavering dedication of young athletes as they compete across various athletic challenges. The M-20 Athletic Meet features an extensive lineup of track and field events, pushing the boundaries of speed, strength, and agility. Athletes represent their departments and colleges, seizing the opportunity to break records, reach personal milestones, and engage in friendly yet intense competition. This meet provides a powerful platform for showcasing skill and determination, exemplifying values like perseverance, teamwork, and fair play.

An inclusive event, the M-20 Meet welcomes athletes at all skill levels, fostering a unified sports culture across the campus. Spectators are equally vital to the event’s spirit, their enthusiasm and support motivating competitors to give their all. This collective energy fills the atmosphere with inspiration and excitement, creating memories that will last long after the final race.

Join us at the Annual M-20 Athletic Sports Meet to witness an unforgettable showcase of resilience, skill, and community spirit, celebrating the very best of RGPV’s athletic talent.

Sport Details & Eligibility

  • Sports Categories:Track & Feild
  • Eligibility: Open to all (both Girls & Boys) registered university students with a valid student ID and Govt. ID.
  • Rules: Participants must adhere to university and national sports federation guidelines.

Venue & Staying Details

Location: REC Campus, 28 Khajuri, MP-Police Training Circuit, Rewa

Please arrive at REC Campus, 28 Khajuri, MP-Police Training Circuit, Rewa at the designated time for check-in and physical verification. All event activities, including matches, results, and the award ceremony, will take place here. Participants must also check out from this location after the event’s conclusion.

Staying Arrangements: Accommodation is available for outstation participants. Nearby hotels and university hostels are available and Booked.

For participants and guests needing accommodations, nearby hotels and guesthouses have been arranged and booked with special rates during the event. Assistance with room number and details will be available at the event registration desk. Please ensure to confirm your stay arrangements upon check-in at REC Campus, 28 Khajuri, MP-Police Training Circuit, Rewa.

Event Timeline

  • Registration Deadline: March 10, 2023
  • Opening Ceremony: March 20, 2023, 10:00 AM
  • Preliminary Rounds: March 21-24, 2025
  • Semi-Finals: March 25, 2023
  • Finals: March 26, 2023
  • Award Ceremony: March 26, 2023, 6:00 PM

Rewards & Prizes

  • Champion: 50,000/- Cash Prize, Trophy & Gold Medal
  • 1st Runner-Up: 30,000/- Cash Prize & Silver Medal
  • 2nd Runner-Up: 10,000/- Cash Prize & Bronze Medal
  • Most-Valuable Player (MVP- 5 Players): 2,000/- Cash Prize & MVP Certificate
  • Fair Play Award (FPA - 5 Players): 2,000/- Cash Prize & FPA Certificate
  • All Participants: Certificate of Participation
