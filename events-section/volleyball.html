Description

The Annual Skand Volleyball Sports Meet is poised to be a showcase of sportsmanship, skill, and competitive spirit, drawing volleyball enthusiasts from across the RGPV community. This much-anticipated event has grown to symbolize unity and athletic excellence, offering a platform for students from diverse disciplines to come together and celebrate their shared passion for sports.

The Skand Volleyball Meet features a range of intense matches that test players' agility, teamwork, and strategic prowess. Each game is more than just a competition—it's an opportunity for participants to push their limits, refine their skills, and engage in healthy rivalry. Athletes in this meet not only represent their respective departments but also embody values such as resilience, discipline, and mutual respect. Every serve, spike, and block contributes to an unforgettable atmosphere, blending intensity with camaraderie, which is at the heart of sports life on campus. The event also promotes an inclusive sports culture, encouraging students of all skill levels to join and experience the thrill of competition. This culture of inclusivity ensures that the Skand Volleyball Meet is as much about community-building as it is about sporting achievement. With enthusiastic spectators cheering from the stands, the energy on the court reaches new heights, driving players to bring their best performances.

Spectators play a crucial role in the Skand Volleyball Meet, creating a vibrant environment that inspires players to elevate their game. Their cheers and support infuse the event with a unique spirit, making it a celebration of collective passion and pride. For players and fans alike, this meet is a time to come together, enjoy thrilling matches, and foster lifelong memories. Join us at the Annual Skand Volleyball Sports Meet for an exciting journey of athletic achievement and shared enthusiasm. Whether participating as a player or cheering from the sidelines, you’ll be part of an unforgettable experience that showcases the best of volleyball and campus camaraderie.

Sport Details & Eligibility

  • Sports Categories:Volleyball
  • Eligibility: Open to all (both Girls & Boys) registered university students with a valid student ID and Govt. ID.
  • Rules: Participants must adhere to university and national sports federation guidelines.

Venue & Staying Details

Location: 952, Sector C, RGPV Stadium, Bhopal

Please arrive at 952, Sector C, RGPV Stadium, Bhopal at the designated time for check-in and physical verification. All event activities, including matches, results, and the award ceremony, will take place here. Participants must also check out from this location after the event’s conclusion.

Staying Arrangements: Accommodation is available for outstation participants. Nearby hotels and university hostels are available and Booked.

For participants and guests needing accommodations, nearby hotels and guesthouses have been arranged and booked with special rates during the event. Assistance with room number and details will be available at the event registration desk. Please ensure to confirm your stay arrangements upon check-in at 952, Sector C, RGPV Stadium, Bhopal.

Event Timeline

  • Registration Deadline: March 10, 2023
  • Opening Ceremony: March 20, 2023, 10:00 AM
  • Preliminary Rounds: March 21-24, 2025
  • Semi-Finals: March 25, 2023
  • Finals: March 26, 2023
  • Award Ceremony: March 26, 2023, 6:00 PM

Rewards & Prizes

  • Champion: 1,20,000/- Cash Prize, Trophy & Gold Medal
  • 1st Runner-Up: 90,000/- Cash Prize & Silver Medal
  • 2nd Runner-Up: 50,000/- Cash Prize & Bronze Medal
  • Most-Valuable Player (MVP- 5 Players): 20,000/- Cash Prize & MVP Certificate
  • Fair Play Award (FPA - 10 Players): 15,000/- Cash Prize & FPA Certificate
  • All Participants: Certificate of Participation
