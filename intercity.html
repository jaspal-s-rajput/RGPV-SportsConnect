Description

The Inter-City Basketball Tournament is set to be one of the most exhilarating sports events of the year, bringing together top basketball talent from various cities for an action-packed showdown at RGPV. Known for its high energy, teamwork, and skill, this tournament is an arena where athletes not only showcase their abilities but also represent their cities with pride and passion. This tournament spans several intense matches, with teams competing in a fast-paced environment that demands agility, precision, and unparalleled focus. Each game is designed to push players to their limits, with every dribble, pass, and shot contributing to the ultimate goal of victory. The competition encourages camaraderie, resilience, and sportsmanship as teams go head-to-head, fostering a culture of respect and admiration among participants and fans alike.

Participants in the Inter-City Basketball Tournament have the unique opportunity to represent their hometowns, showcasing their commitment to both the sport and their communities. With well-organized schedules and a supportive atmosphere, the tournament is an ideal platform for players to challenge themselves, sharpen their skills, and experience the thrill of competitive basketball. Spectators play a vital role in this tournament, bringing infectious enthusiasm that fuels the intensity of each match. Their cheers and support provide players with the motivation to give their best and contribute to an electrifying environment that resonates throughout the stadium. The Inter-City Basketball Tournament is not just a series of games—it’s a celebration of athletic spirit, teamwork, and city pride.

Join us for the Inter-City Basketball Tournament at AITR and witness an unforgettable display of talent, dedication, and unity. Whether as a player or a fan, come be part of an exciting journey that highlights the passion and skill of basketball at its finest.

Sport Details & Eligibility

  • Sports Categories:Basketball
  • Eligibility: Open to all (both Girls & Boys) registered university students with a valid student ID and Govt. ID.
  • Rules: Participants must adhere to university and national sports federation guidelines.

Venue & Staying Details

Location: AITR Main Ground, Indore

Please arrive at AITR Main Ground, Indore at the designated time for check-in and physical verification. All event activities, including matches, results, and the award ceremony, will take place here. Participants must also check out from this location after the event’s conclusion.

Staying Arrangements: Accommodation is available for outstation participants. Nearby hotels and university hostels are available and Booked.

For participants and guests needing accommodations, nearby hotels and guesthouses have been arranged and booked with special rates during the event. Assistance with room number and details will be available at the event registration desk. Please ensure to confirm your stay arrangements upon check-in at AITR Main Ground, Indore.

Event Timeline

  • Registration Deadline: March 10, 2025
  • Opening Ceremony: March 20, 2025, 10:00 AM
  • Preliminary Rounds: March 21-24, 2025
  • Semi-Finals: March 25, 2025
  • Finals: March 26, 2025
  • Award Ceremony: March 26, 2025, 6:00 PM

Rewards & Prizes

  • Champion:80,000/- Cash Prize, Trophy & Gold Medal
  • 1st Runner-Up: 40,000/- Cash Prize & Silver Medal
  • 2nd Runner-Up: 20,000/- Cash Prize & Bronze Medal
  • Most-Valuable Player (MVP- 3 Players): 5,000/- Cash Prize & MVP Certificate
  • Fair Play Award (FPA - 5 Players): 3,000/- Cash Prize & FPA Certificate
  • All Participants: Certificate of Participation
